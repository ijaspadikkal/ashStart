About Us

Aparna Healthcare Solutions LLP was incorporated in Kerala in September 2020 by Mr. Premarajan its Managing Partner and his wife Bindu is Designated Partner. Aparna Surgical is the digital part of Aparna Healthcare Solutions LLP. We inform the proper awareness of Aparna Healthcare Solutions LLP through aparnasurgical.com. In the year 2022 April Onwards, Mr. Kumaran also becomes a Partner of the Company.

aparnasurgical.com offers scientifically proven disinfectants and cleaning solutions to reduce harmful effects on patients and the environment manufactured by MICROGEN HYGIENE PVT LTD. Their products and our services protect people against infections and prevent medical equipment from contamination. Aparna Healthcare Solutions LLP is one of the pioneering institutions in Health care service providers in South India. We are a leading distributor of healthcare products and are committed to serving healthcare communities.

We aim to ensure exceptional customer service by reducing cancellations of orders due to short supplies and providing prompt service and care. We are the leaders in the dealer network backed by efficient planning, a dedicated team of sales and marketing professionals, and experts in marketing and distribution of Medical Equipment, Hospital consumables, Surgical, Disposable, and other Health care products. aparnasurgical.com moving ahead with a vision to match competitors by adding channel partners with value-added brands, emphasizing the quality of products and services delivered.

Premarajan

MD of Aparna Health Care Solution LLP

In my 14 years of experience in the surgical field, I noticed a great problem that affected many people and that is the unavailability of important medical supplies to them. For example, cancer patients from my area at times did not find colostomy bags after their surgeries at RCC Trivandrum; they neither knew where to buy them nor did they even consider going back to TVM to buy them. Equally, patients with varicose veins from 2010-2015 were not familiar with compression stockings' importance. These experiences encouraged me to start an Aparna Surgical business committed to addressing these gaps and providing holistic support to lay persons. Towards making this dream come true, I brought together a team of professionals who had high-level knowledge in technology and products and they formed the timber beam of our mission.
