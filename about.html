Premarajan

CEO of Aparna Health Care Solution LLP

After completion of my B-Com Graduation I was joined with Transport Corporation of India Ltd., as an Accounts Assistant at their Allahabad Branch in 1986. TCI Ltd is one of the largest Transport Company in Asia. As I was planning to change my carrier from service industry to product industry I left my service with TCI.

After 10 years service in various capacities on different branches of TCI, I was resigned and joined with M/S Eastern Fibre Industries Ltd., as an Accountant at Ahmedabad in the year 1997.

After six years in service in Eastern Fibere Industries Ltd., Ahmadabad I was joined with Sabar Pumps Pvt Ltd., Ahmedabad during 1997 as a Branch Manager of their Pune Branch. From Pune I was transferred to Bangalore Branch during 2005 and from 2005 to 2009 I was in Bangalore and decided to stop outstation life, so I resigned from Sabar Pumps and settled in home town.

From the year 2010 onwards I was joined hand with Hospicare Group Companies, it’s a proprietorship company and the proprietor was Mr.A.O.Krishnan dealing with disposable surgical itmes and equipments. We started HYGIENECARE in Calicut in the year 2010 and I take over its Manager post for last 10 years [06.10.2020] Then I realized that I have nothing to do more for Hygienecare and to be built-up a new own firm I was started APARNA HEALTHCARE SOLUTIONS LLP.

This my 35 Year’s Journey of carrier life from 1985 to 2020.
