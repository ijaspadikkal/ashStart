About Us

Aparna Healthcare Solutions LLP was incorporated in Kerala in September 2020 by Mr. Premarajan its Managing Partner and his wife Bindu is Designated Partner. Aparna Surgical is the digital part of Aparna Healthcare Solutions LLP. We inform the proper awareness of Aparna Healthcare Solutions LLP through aparnasurgical.com. In the year 2022 April Onwards, Mr. Kumaran also becomes a Partner of the Company.

aparnasurgical.com offers scientifically proven disinfectants and cleaning solutions to reduce harmful effects on patients and the environment manufactured by MICROGEN HYGIENE PVT LTD. Their products and our services protect people against infections and prevent medical equipment from contamination. Aparna Healthcare Solutions LLP is one of the pioneering institutions in Health care service providers in South India. We are a leading distributor of healthcare products and are committed to serving healthcare communities.

We aim to ensure exceptional customer service by reducing cancellations of orders due to short supplies and providing prompt service and care. We are the leaders in the dealer network backed by efficient planning, a dedicated team of sales and marketing professionals, and experts in marketing and distribution of Medical Equipment, Hospital consumables, Disposable Surgicals, and other Health care products. aparnasurgical.com moving ahead with a vision to match competitors by adding channel partners with value-added brands, emphasizing the quality of products and services delivered.

Premarajan

MD of Aparna Health Care Solution LLP

During my 14 years of work experience in the disposable surgical trading field, I noticed a great problems, that many cancer patients or their bystanders are unaware of the availability of colostomy bags to be used after surgery. Many of them from my surrounding area like the interior areas of Malappuram and Calicut districts went to Trivandrum to buy these bags. I met with a lot of varicose vein patients who didn’t know the importance of compression stockings and how to wear them.

On seeing this kind of shocking and sympathetic situation faced by the rural public, I got the idea to start a disposable surgical store to be able to get emergency medical devices at a reasonable price to the patient. As a result, Aparna Healthcare Solutions LLP was formed in 2020.
